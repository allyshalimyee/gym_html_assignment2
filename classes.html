Our Classes

We have a wide variety of classes to cater to your fitness needs. Our classes are called the IDK series of classes as we believe that everyone should have a chance to learn, even though "I Don't Know (IDK)" anything. So come and join us and our experienced instructors in removing the "D" from "IDK"!

IDK How to Fly (Aerial Yoga)

Want to experience flight? Relaxing in mid-air? This is the class for you! Learn relaxation techniques and how to suspend in the air using our specialised aerial yoga equipment, imported from the United Kingdom.

IDK How to Swim Classes

Wannabe Joseph Schooling for an hour? Come join our swimming lessons. We have lesson for adults, children and even senior citizens. Our swimming instructors are all trained in water safety and there is always a lifeguard on deck. Come and join us for a dip in one of Singapore's few indoor swimming pools!

IDK How to Dance Classes

From K-pop Dances to Latin Dancing and Ballroom Dancing, there is a dance for everyone! Be as cool at BTS at our Kpop class or learn the salsa at our Latin Dance class. You can even imagine yourself a prince or princess at our Ballroom Dancing class. Anyone can Dance at our IDK How to Dance series of classes!

IDK How to Pilates Classes

Come and improve your strength, flexibility and posture here at our Pilates Classes! Get them lean muscles all in our one hour sessions. Our instructors are patient and are willing to help you along in your pilates journey.
